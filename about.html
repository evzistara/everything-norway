ABOUT

Hi!

My name is Evzi and I moved to Norway in 2020. I thought the process was kinda draining with trying to figure out what permit do I need, how do I open a bank account, what the hell is BankID and why is it utmost difficult to get a norwegian sim card.

But I really love to research stuff and I also like to share knowledge and help out other people. There is absolutely no reason the process of moving being so difficult. We have to worry about leaving our old life behind and starting the new one and stressing about what kind of papers to fill out shouldn’t be the main focus.

That’s why I decided to create this webpage together with a Tik Tok account, where I try to explain everything you need when you move to Norway in a simple and understandable way.

There is obviously a lot of info for different situations and I will try to gather everything possible so this website is a living and growing thing. Please bear with me while I am gathering all the info.

If you are missing anything specific, let me know on the email below!

With love,

Evzi
